Jim's Pizza

Jim's Pizza is a a cozy neighborhood spot serving up classic Neapolitan-style pizzas since 1992. Established by Jim Smith, who had a passion for authentic Italian cuisine, the pizza quickly gained a reputation for its flavorful pies and welcoming atmosphere. Over the years, Jim's has become a local favorite, attracting generations of families and friends! Enjoy freshly baked pies topped with high-quality ingredients and a variety of unique flavor combinations!
